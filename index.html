Welcome to Atoiks Games.

Note: If the following games asks for administrator priveliges on Windows, please right click > Properties > Unblock > Ok. That should solve the problem.

Die or Rage Original

The first game of the DOR (Die or Rage) series. Play as Ryan, a man on a mission to rescue his wife from the depths of hell, in this 2d action platformer adventure. Fight bosses, solve puzzles and pilot drones to accomplish your goals. Play it here.

Die or rage NM

Was Die or Rage too easy? Well fear not for this game if meant for you. This game is comprised of all the Die or Rage levels modified to be 10 times as hard. Although not a sequel to Die or Rage, each level promises to be a challenge. Can you help Ryan? Play it here.

Jarg

Jarg is a two person battle game where the players face off in a variety of exciting arenas. Each player controls a square that can move and teleport across the map as they attempt to shoot the other square. Play it here.

Snark

Snark: (define Snark - v. To snort...). OR (define Snark - n. What happends when a lazer shark fights against poison breathing snakes in a jungle temple.). In this game you play as the shark and the snakes are your enemy. The goal of the game is to destroy the fireballs that are shot from the snakes while using as few shots as possible to get a highscore. Bit Blaster

Ever heard of the famous Space Invaders? This is a similar version of it written in C# using Windows Form technology. Go through a multitude of levels, dodging bullets and blasting bits! Available on Windows as of now! Download it here.

Keyboard King

These games are fastpaced keyboard clickers. Play both modes to prove you're the keyboard king! Available on Windows as of now! Download it here.

Cloned Cards

Who has the fastest reflexes? Click the key when the cards match to earn points and beat your friends! Available on Windows as of now! Download it here.
